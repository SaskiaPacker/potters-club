About us

Start your journey:

Potters club is an independent ceramics studio. Holding ceramic workshops for everyone from beginner to advance level. We'll help you every step of the way and show you all of the different techniques used to help make every type of pottery. All you need is to bring is yourself, your creative juices and a glass or two of something if wish. We'll do the rest.
