Classes

Beginner

These classes will use more simplistic methods to begin with and teach you a some theory, so you understand why things are done, not just how. This is to engage you with the practise of ceramics and hopefully give you a good basis for understanding different methods of ceramics. We will coax out your creativity skills, allowing you to see the progress over the weeks. Typically students will come to this class for around 12 weeks and then move on to intermediate.

Intermediate

This is where we start to introduce you to things like using a wheel, where you can make pretty much anything. We will build upon the skills learned in the beginner course (if you're coming in straight into intermediate class, then that's not a problem at all, as you will already have a good understanding and skills; we can help you build on those). We will aim to make a range of pieces in this class and introduce you to different decorative, experimental methods. This class is all about being experimental and exploring to find what you love. Students usually come to this class for around 8 weeks.

Advanced

You will have a great understanding of the practise by this point and will be very confident. Here we really start to refine your skills and allow you to take the reins on your own projects. We'll continue to expand your knowledge on the making and decorating part. In addition, we'll build your knowledge and start to get you to think of yourself as your own artist. We'll show you where you can start to sell your work, if that's something you wish to do, really starting to get you to think of yourself as a brand. People usually come to this class for around 10 weeks.
